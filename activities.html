Activities

Mangrove Tour

Pulau Langkawi, known for its stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage, offers a wide array of activities that cater to all types of travelers. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast, a history buff, a nature lover, or someone seeking relaxation, Langkawi has something to offer. The island's diverse activities range from thrilling outdoor adventures and serene beach experiences to insightful cultural tours and lively nightlife.

Sky Bridge Walk

It is a 125-meter long curved pedestrian bridge located 660 meters above sea level at the peak of Gunung Mat Cincang.

Completed in 2005, it is considered the world's longest free-span and curved bridge, offering stunning panoramic views of the Andaman Sea, rainforests, and surrounding islands.

Visitors can reach the bridge by taking the Langkawi Cable Car to the top station, and then either hiking down a 150m trail or riding the SkyGlide inclined elevator to the bridge entrance.

Cable Car Ride

It is a gondola lift that takes visitors from the Oriental Village up to the peak of Gunung Mat Cincang, the second highest mountain in Langkawi.

The cable car ride offers stunning panoramic views of the lush rainforests, cascading waterfalls, sparkling Andaman Sea, and surrounding islands.

The journey covers a distance of 2.2km and takes visitors up to an elevation of 708 meters above sea level.

Jetskiing

Prices for jet ski rentals in Langkawi generally range from RM200-RM400 per hour, depending on the operator and number of riders.

Many jet ski tour packages also include access to other attractions like the private Paradise 101 island, which has its own aqua park and facilities.

Jet skiing is best enjoyed during the dry season from January to April when the seas are calmer.

Island Hopping

These tours typically visit 3-4 different islands, often including highlights like:

  • Maiden Island (Pulau Dayang Bunting) - Known for its freshwater lake and scenic landscapes

  • Singa Besar Island - Site of an eagle feeding station where you can witness majestic eagles swooping down to catch prey

  • Beras Basah Island - Offers opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxing on the beach

Many jet ski tour packages also include access to other attractions like the private Paradise 101 island, which has its own aqua park and facilities.

Jet skiing is best enjoyed during the dry season from January to April when the seas are calmer.
