Attractions

Natures & Adventures

Whether you are seeking adventure, relaxation, historical insights, or family-friendly activities, Langkawi has something to offer. The island's diverse attractions range from pristine beaches and lush rainforests to intriguing cultural landmarks and modern entertainment facilities. Here is a closer look at the various sections of attractions that make Pulau Langkawi a must-visit destination:

Gunung Raya

The highest mountain in Langkawi at 881m, with a tower and viewing platform at the summit.

Kilim Karst Geoforst Park

A series of seven pools and waterfalls in the rainforest, with a scenic hiking trail.

Telaga Tujuh Waterfalls

A mangrove forest with limestone cliffs, caves, and diverse wildlife like walking fish, kingfishers, and otters

Tanjung Rhu Beach

The beach is known for its tranquil and secluded atmosphere, away from the crowds. Visitors can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and watching the stunning sunsets over the Andaman Sea.

Crocodile Adventureland

The largest crocodile park in Langkawi, with crocodile feeding shows

Langkawi Night Market

Langkawi is home to several vibrant night markets that offer a unique cultural experience for visitors.Features over 50 stalls selling a variety of local cuisine, souvenirs, clothing and more

Pulau Payar Marine Park

An island with excellent snorkeling and diving among coral reefs

Langkawi Arts in Paradise 3D Museum

An interactive 3D art museum

Dataran Lang (Eagle Square)

Features a large eagle statue representing Langkawi's name, which means "reddish-brown eagle" in Malay
